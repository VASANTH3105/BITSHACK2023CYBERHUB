Beginner Level Cybersecurity Learning Path

  • Stage: 1
    Introduction to Cybersecurity
    Learn the fundamental concepts of cybersecurity, including the importance of security, common threats, and basic terminology.
  • Stage: 2
    Networking and Protocols
    Dive into networking basics, understand protocols, and explore how data is transmitted securely. Learn about firewalls and intrusion detection systems.
  • Stage: 3
    Operating Systems and Security
    Explore operating system security, focusing on Windows and Linux. Learn about user authentication, access controls, and securing system configurations.
  • Stage: 4
    Cybersecurity Policies and Compliance
    Understand cybersecurity policies, regulations, and compliance standards. Explore risk management and incident response planning.
  • Stage: 5
    Threats and Defense Strategies
    Delve into advanced threats such as malware, phishing, and ransomware. Learn about cybersecurity best practices, defense strategies, and tools.
