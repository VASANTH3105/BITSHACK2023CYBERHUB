An attacker identifies a coffee shop with an open Wi-Fi network. Customers at the coffee shop are required to enter a password to connect to the network. The attacker waits nearby and watches as a customer enters the password to access the Wi-Fi. Once the customer is connected, the attacker immediately connects to the same network using the stolen password, effectively piggybacking on the legitimate user's access. The attacker can then attempt to launch cyber attacks or intercept sensitive data on the network.

Honey Trap Game

You meet someone online who claims to be a U.S. military service member stationed overseas. They send you attractive photos and express their love for you. They ask for your help and request that you send them a gift, cash, or even cryptocurrency to show your love. What will you do?
